Biography:
If someone was to ask me what I did as a kid, my answer would be "drawing and surfing online". Most adults consider this a waste of time, but to me, it was a period of expanding my mind beyound the rectangular realm that I call home. I was not simply doodling or wasting my time online, but I was traveling off to a place where my physical body cannot reach. It was a time when I was actively making my characters come alive and preparing visual notes so that my sibling and I could act them out. Curiosity and imagination had been a big part of my life and will stay even after graduation in the next semester. After the last year of college, I hope to be a part of the User Experience field so that not only can I contribute but also to learn more about it.
